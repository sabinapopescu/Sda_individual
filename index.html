Our Services

Car loan calculator

A car loan is a type of financing that allows you to borrow money to purchase a vehicle, which you repay with interest over a set period.

Home loan calculator

A home loan, also known as a mortgage, is a loan specifically used to finance the purchase of a home. You repay the loan with interest over a long period, typically 15-30 years.

Business loan calculator

A business loan provides financial resources to a company for various purposes, such as starting a business, expanding operations, or purchasing equipment.

Personal loan calculator

A personal loan is an unsecured loan that offers a lump sum of cash for a variety of personal expenses, such as debt consolidation, home renovations, or unexpected bills.

Amortization table

An amortization table is a breakdown of your loan payments over time, showing how much of each payment goes towards interest and how much goes towards reducing the principal amount owed.

Student loan calculator

A student loan is a type of loan specifically designed to help students finance the cost of higher education, including tuition, fees, and living expenses.
